An Empty Promise

Raging Winds

Will I make it to the next day?…Will I survive to see the stars aline once more across the night? Can you be there with me when the sky goes black… *Holds a trinket to their chest* Can you be here with me when it gets cold…?

I have so much to do, and not enough time. We had so much more to do….More smiles to be had…More life to bind..I don’t want to leave yet, I haven’t done enough. I want to rummage the fields in bliss once more with you. Dine the night away with vigor while the room is filled to the brim with laughter. To swing to the non-existent tune with your warmth in my arms. The days of bliss that I wish we still had. *A Trembling sigh and becomes more dry* I'm sorry for not doing enough…I’m sorry that I’m not going to be there to sing you one more lullaby.

I will live on as long as I can to see the day's end, and make it home to you. There, waiting on the porch to embrace me tightly, releasing the unwavering sorrow we’ve once had. I love you with the deepest part of my soul, I will never forget you. If I don’t return, please don’t forget me while you move on as selfish as I am. *Chuckles into a sigh* I miss you.

This is a story about a person dying and is thinking of his loved ones as his life slowly fades.
