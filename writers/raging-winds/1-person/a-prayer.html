A Prayer

Raging Winds

Oh how the gods have given me such grace. The blessing upon my soul with the satisfaction of warmth and fulfillment during these times of great pain. FOR THE GODS HAVE BLESSED ME WITH TACO BELL ON THIS FINE NIGHT. My belly will forever be filled by God's grace bestowed upon me. The succulent crunch of that dorito taco, the warm balls of goo that engulf my cheeks with its sugary, cinnamon goodness. What a blessing this is on a late friday night. For tomorrow, I will endure a battle far beyond any man or woman. For this time of blessing I am given, I will fuel myself for the battle of bloodshed and tears for I have succumbed. The battle will be gruesome, but I will be victorious.
