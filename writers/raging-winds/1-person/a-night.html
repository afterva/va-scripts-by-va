A Night

Raging Winds

The stars beam within the sea of the endless blanket of darkness that covers our sky. It reminds me of the first night my eyes have ever shined on you. It was like a shooting star had fallen into my chest as you walked side by side with me. A night like this is one to cherish for as long as I can remember.

The warmth of your hand intertwined with mine, the smile oh so gently blossoms towards me with genuine joy. *Inhales softly* It's a shame that the time we have won't last as long as I’d wish for, but there will come another day where we will have this moment again. Maybe we could even go out and have a nice dinner or go shopping till your heart explodes, of course free of charge *chuckles* Oh! And we could even sneak into a cinema together! Life looks as if we have an eternity to do things with each other. There's no rush to do everything in one night, right? I’ve got the rest of my life to spend with you.
