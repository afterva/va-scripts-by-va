A Voice

I don’t understand. I just want to feel everyone's joy but I can't stand to be around anyone. I just feel like no one understands me, and it's hard…It's just so..hard. I don’t complain. I just listen to everyone, bickering about their lives, while I just kick rocks and stare at the ground. *Kicks some pebbles into the water and sighs* Maybe I’m not worth it to live a life to the fullest.

Maybe..maybe I’m just here to listen to everyone's problems and carry on with my day. It feels as though no one cares to hear what I say. I have a voice too damnit…*looks into the water* No, I don’t.. *Swipes away at their reflection* Why would someone as small as me have such a thing..? I don’t deserve to have a hand out like that. Who cares if the person that hears your rants have anything to say…I-I don’t need any..thing *Tears up and bundles into a ball* Why can’t I have what they have…why..why am I stuck with nothing…not even a single thanks. I listen to everyone's stories... but why does no one listen to mine?
